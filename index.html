Our Story

according to Joe Diamond

The year was 2008.

It was a year about money, politics and change. But young Thomas Alter didn't care about all of that. A new Nickelback album had just been released, and Tommy and his friends were enjoying themselves at the local pub. As he sipped on his tall, cool Budweiser, he noticed a fine gaggle of ladies several tables over. Tommy gathered his courage, his wingmen and a couple extra brewskis. He then made, what some would say, the smoothest move ever made at a bar, by anyone.

"Sup", said young Tommy.

Noticing his beautiful long Fabio-like hair, and large, thick man-beard, a woman that can only be described as Nicole, met his gaze. Their eyes remained locked, for what seemed like an eternity, as their friends became more and more uncomfortable. To the untrained eye, Nicole was clearly out of his league. She was hip, stylish and well-groomed...all the things that Tommy was not. Yet, for reasons still unknown to this day, Nicole was drawn to the beard/humor combo that comprises Tommy's personality.

That night came and went. Confused by these strange feelings for Tommy, and consumed with the desire to become more hip and more stylish, Nicole left for Italy....for a year.

Some say that Nicole's return to America, a year later, is the most important event in the the story of their relationship....which is true. Her friendship with Tommy was re-kindled, and they ended up vacationing at the Alter's beach house in the Outer Banks. In a magical week filled with sand, surf, and sunsets...surrounded by body hair and Smirnoff Ice, Tommy and Nicole kissed for the very first time.

And that's the story of Tommy and Nicole's first kiss.

Since that night, they have been inseparable. They have built a home, friends, and family around the love they have for each other. This July, as we witness their wedding, I couldn't be happier that they found each other, and I couldn't be more honored to call them both my family.

- Joe
